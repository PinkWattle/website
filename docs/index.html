About Pink Wattle

Friendly, practical therapy in the places that matter

We’re a Perth-based team of experienced occupational therapists who believe therapy works best in real-life settings. Rather than asking you to come to a clinic, we come to you — meeting you in your home, school, workplace or community.

From early childhood through to adulthood, we support people of all ages and abilities across the full breadth of occupational therapy. Whether you’re funded through the NDIS or arranging private therapy, you’ll work with a therapist who takes the time to understand your goals and build a plan around them.

What we do

Occupational therapy across all disciplines

Our therapists support a wide range of needs and goals. Here are some of the areas we work in — if you don’t see what you’re looking for, just ask.

Paediatric OT

Supporting children to build the skills they need for play, learning and everyday independence — at home, in childcare and at school.

Adult & adolescent therapy

Practical, goal-focused support for teens and adults to participate confidently in daily life, study, work and the community.

Daily living skills

Building independence in self-care, routines, cooking and the everyday activities that matter most to you and your family.

Sensory & regulation support

Strategies to understand sensory needs and support emotional regulation across home, school and work environments.

Assistive technology & equipment

Assessment, prescription and trials of equipment and assistive technology to make everyday tasks safer and easier.

Home & environment assessments

Reviewing your spaces and recommending modifications that improve safety, access and independence.

Who we help
